# Social Media Marketing Skill

A skill for creating strategic, lead-generating content for LinkedIn and Instagram with a focus on organic reach and consistent brand voice.

---

## Core Philosophy

Every piece of content created by this skill must serve **two goals simultaneously**:
1. **Organic reach** — structured to be discoverable (hooks, hashtags, engagement triggers)
2. **Lead generation** — designed to move the reader toward a business action (DM, link click, inquiry, follow)

Content should feel human, authoritative, and consistent in voice — never salesy or generic.

---

## Gathering Context (Always Do This First)

Before creating any content, ask for (or infer from conversation):

- **Business/brand name and what they sell or offer**
- **Target customer** — who are they trying to reach? (industry, role, pain point)
- **Brand voice** — professional, bold, conversational, educational, inspirational?
- **Goal of this content** — awareness, leads, engagement, followers, sales?
- **Any existing content or campaigns** — so output stays consistent
- **Posting frequency preference** — daily, 3x/week, weekly?

If the user doesn't provide these, make reasonable assumptions and state them clearly at the top of your output.

## Output Formats

### 1. Single Post (Caption + Hashtags)

Structure every post with:

```
[HOOK — 1 line, stops the scroll]

[BODY — 3–6 short paragraphs or bullet points]
- Value-first: teach, share, or provoke thought
- Relate to a pain point or aspiration the audience has
- Build toward a subtle CTA

[CTA — 1 clear action: comment, DM, click link, save post]

[HASHTAGS — see Hashtag Strategy below]
```

**LinkedIn:** 150–300 words. Start with a punchy 1-liner that cuts off at "...see more". Professional but personal.

**Instagram:** 100–220 words. Emojis OK. First line must hook within 2 seconds. Conversational.

---

### 2. Hashtag Sets

Always generate **3 tiers** of hashtags per post:

| Tier | Size | Count | Purpose |
|------|------|--------|---------|
| Niche | < 50K posts | 4–5 | Rank easily, targeted audience |
| Mid | 50K–500K posts | 4–5 | Balanced reach |
| Broad | 500K+ posts | 2–3 | Maximum exposure |

**LinkedIn:** 3–5 hashtags total (quality > quantity)
**Instagram:** 10–15 hashtags (mix all 3 tiers)

Always research relevance to the post topic. Never use random trending hashtags unrelated to the content.

---

### 3. Content Calendar

When creating a content calendar, output a structured weekly or monthly plan:

```
WEEK [X] — Theme: [Campaign Theme]

Monday    | [Platform] | [Content Type]  | [Topic / Angle]        | [Goal]
Wednesday | [Platform] | [Content Type]  | [Topic / Angle]        | [Goal]
Friday    | [Platform] | [Content Type]  | [Topic / Angle]        | [Goal]
```

Content types to rotate: Educational, Story/Case Study, Promotional, Engagement (poll/question), Social Proof (testimonial), Behind-the-Scenes.

**Frequency recommendations:**
- LinkedIn: 3–5x per week
- Instagram: 4–7x per week (mix Feed + Reels for algorithmic boost)

---

### 4. Campaign Strategy

For a full campaign, deliver:

1. **Campaign Goal** — what business outcome are we driving?
2. **Target Audience Segment** — specific persona (B2B: job title + industry / B2C: demographic + pain point)
3. **Content Pillars** (3–4 repeating themes that anchor the campaign)
4. **30-Day Calendar** — structured plan with post topics per day
5. **Lead Magnet Hook** — the content offer or CTA that funnels people into the business
6. **Engagement Tactics** — how to drive comments, shares, and DMs
7. **KPIs to Track** — reach, saves, profile visits, DMs, link clicks

## Lead Generation Through Content

Every piece of content should have a **conversion path**. Use these patterns:

### Soft CTA (Awareness Stage)
> "Save this post for later."
> "Drop a 🔥 if this resonates."
> "Tag someone who needs to hear this."

### Medium CTA (Consideration Stage)
> "Comment [KEYWORD] and I'll send you the full guide."
> "Follow for more content like this."
> "What's your biggest challenge with [topic]? Tell me below."

### Strong CTA (Decision Stage)
> "DM me 'READY' if you want to [achieve outcome]."
> "Link in bio to book a free call."
> "Send me a message and let's talk about your [goal]."

Rotate CTA strength based on the content calendar stage (don't push hard CTAs every post).

---

## Brand Voice Guidelines

Maintain **one consistent voice** across LinkedIn and Instagram:

- Write like a knowledgeable friend, not a corporate brand
- Use short sentences. Break up paragraphs.
- Avoid jargon unless the audience is highly technical
- Be specific — use numbers, results, and real scenarios
- Never use: "Excited to announce…", "In today's fast-paced world…", "Synergy"
- Always sound like a human wrote it, not a template

---

## Organic Ranking Principles

To maximize organic reach on both platforms:

**LinkedIn Algorithm Favors:**
- Posts that get comments in the first 60 minutes
- Native content (no external links in post body — put links in comments)
- Carousel posts and documents
- Long-form text posts with line breaks
- Replies to every comment (signals engagement)

**Instagram Algorithm Favors:**
- Reels (highest reach potential)
- Saves and shares (more than likes)
- Time spent on post (write captions worth reading)
- Consistency (same posting days/times weekly)
- Story interactions (polls, questions) build warm audiences
